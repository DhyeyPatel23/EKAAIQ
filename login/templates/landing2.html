Create and Host Quizzes

Easily design custom quizzes featuring multiple-choice questions tailored to your needs. Whether for educational purposes, training sessions, or fun challenges, create quizzes with a user-friendly interface.

AI-Generated Quiz Questions

Harness the power of our advanced AI to effortlessly generate multiple-choice questions tailored to your chosen topic. Simply input your subject or area of interest, and our intelligent system will automatically create a set of well-crafted questions, complete with options.

Join Quizzes with a Room Code

Joining a quiz has never been easier! Simply enter the unique 6-digit code shared by the host, and you’ll be instantly connected to the quiz, ready to compete. Whether you're participating in a live event, a classroom session.

Editable Quizzes

Easily modify your existing quizzes on the fly with our intuitive editing tools. Whether you need to update questions, adjust correct answers, or change quiz details, our platform makes the process smooth and hassle-free. Whether you spot an error after publishing or want to refresh your quiz with new content, you can make changes in real-time without any disruptions.

Secure OTP Email Verification

Guarantee secure access to your quizzes with our robust email verification system, featuring One-Time Password (OTP) authentication. Before participants can join a quiz, they are required to verify their identity through an email-based OTP system. This added layer of security ensures that only authorized users can participate, preventing unauthorized access and maintaining the integrity of your quiz.
